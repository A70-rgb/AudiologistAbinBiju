Professional Journey

Abin Biju's career has been defined by a commitment to the most vulnerable populations. He completed 4 impactful years at the Kusumagiri Mental Health Center in Ernakulam, a premier institution for psychological and speech-related rehabilitation. Here, he honed his skills in diagnosing and treating complex communication disorders in a multidisciplinary environment.

Furthering his reach, his tenure at Padashala in Alappuzha allowed him to focus on pediatric rehabilitation and early intervention strategies, ensuring children have the support they need to develop vital communication skills.

"My philosophy is rooted in the belief that therapy is most effective when it is customized to the patient's individual lifestyle, emotional well-being, and family goals."

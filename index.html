Meet Abin Biju (BASLP) Audiologist & Speech Therapist

With over 4+ years of clinical experience, Abin Biju has dedicated his career to improving the communication abilities of individuals across all age groups.

Having served at esteemed institutions like Kusumagiri Mental Health Centre and Padashala, he brings a wealth of specialized knowledge in both rehabilitative audiology and speech pathology.

His approach combines clinical excellence with compassionate, patient-centered care, ensuring that every individual receives a therapy plan as unique as their own voice.
